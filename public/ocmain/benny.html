Benny is just your average guy. He has a life that some others see as mildly boring, but normal to him (like almost everybody). He usually sticks to his usual schedule and barely makes any alternations to his schedule, although he does frequently meet his friends, head on to downtown, do shopping, or maybe all three at once. Benny is a very chill guy, not a lot bothers him and he's very easy going. Benny is not mad very frequently, but more frequent compared to once of his friends, Nix. Benny often prefers to be alone sometimes when he's doing all of his favorite things, and when he's hanging out with others, he tries to join in and sometimes joke around.
